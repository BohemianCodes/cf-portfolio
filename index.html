The Long Story

I spent many years of my life traveling. 2011, at the age of 27, I embarked on a colorful long-term odysee that satisfied my heart's longings and transformed into a profound inwards journey as well. Since the, I visited 21 countries on 4 continents- but, I never had intentions collecting stamps or breaking records. I have re-visited places many times, like my beloved Southern Mexico. I always travelled on a shoestring, just making enough money on the go.

Then, In 2014, I set foot onto the fair island of Ireland. I fell in love at once. It was to unfold as a beautiful relationship. I lost my heart to the wild west of the Emerald Isle- I must admit, she was always very kind to me, lifted me up when I was down, with her blue skies and sheepy clouds, magnificent wild landscapes and magical water's edges to contemplate into the nature of life.

Now, after six years, I feel very happy to have had so many beautiful experiences around the world, and I did start to feel it was time for another profound change. The times of budget traveling, sleeping in hammocks and picking cherries are but beautiful memories. I might even say, it was time to grow up a little, find a well-paying work I am passionate about, and a way to connect with the world that is so rapidly evolving all around.

In the light of this change, I moved back to Berlin, the city I had left in 2011 to travel the world. All colorful experiences live inside me, and I enjoy my return to Berlin and my path to living my passion as a web developer.
